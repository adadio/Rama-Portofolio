About Me

Hi there! I’m Syaputra Rama Adadio, or you can call me Rama — an Informatics Engineering graduate who loves combining creativity and technology to build something meaningful. I enjoy crafting digital experiences that are not only functional but also visually engaging.

During my journey, I’ve worked on various projects such as an Automatic Catfish Feeder IoT System using ESP32 and Blynk, and a Todo List web app built with HTML, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript. I also have experience in creative media, including event documentation, digital banner design, and developing a dynamic school website using WordPress. Each project has helped me grow into a more adaptive, detail-oriented, and curious learner.

I’m passionate about learning new technologies, especially in web development and system integration. My goal is to keep improving, keep building, and keep creating solutions that make everyday life a little better step by step, code by code.
